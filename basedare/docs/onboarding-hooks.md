## The 14-day ignition plan

**Phase 0 — before any posting (this week)**
- [ ] Android + desktop QA pass (gate for promoting the 60-second flow)
- [ ] Register @basedare handles (X/IG/TikTok) — park them, founder account posts
- [ ] Drill night: 1 friend, 1 bar, full loop. Fix stumbles.
- [ ] Screenshot bank: map at night, receipt PNG, crown on a pin, reel playing

**Phase 1 — the mates (days 1–4)**
- Group chat sequence (5 messages, already drafted in feature-guide era):
  tease receipt → 60-second ask → land grab → anti-tinder line → Friday plan
- Goal: 5–10 claimed tags, everyone checked in somewhere once
- First X post: option A ("been quiet. been building.") + map screenshot

**Phase 2 — the land grab goes public (days 5–9)**
- Post hook #11 with the real unproven-venue count; recount publicly as it drops
- Post the first crown: "👑 first mayor on the island: @[tag] at [venue]"
- Venue pitch #1: walk into Hideaway with THEIR proof trail on your phone
  (hook #16). Ask for nothing but the claim. Free = no decision needed.

**Phase 3 — the night (days 10–14)**
- Friday at Hideaway: tags at the table, crossed paths light up live,
  one funded dare mid-night (someone gets PAID — film the reaction)
- Same night: everyone shares receipts to stories (the ad wave)
- Next morning: recap post — receipts, the crown standing, reel replay
- Venue pitch #2 uses Friday's receipts as the deck. "This happened at
  Hideaway. Want Thursday?"

**Cadence after ignition:** 3 posts/week max, artifact-led only —
Monday: weekly crown/heat recap · Wednesday: land-grab countdown ·
Friday/Sat: live receipts. Never post without a real screenshot.

## Venue-owner pitch script (60 seconds, in person)

1. Open the map, show THEIR venue: "this is you. these are verified
   check-ins — GPS + QR, can't be faked."
2. Show a receipt: "every one of these gets shared with your name sealed
   in gold on it."
3. Show the crown: "your best regular is literally your mayor. people
   fight over this."
4. The ask: "claiming is free — takes 2 minutes, you see who's coming
   and when. that's it. no catch, no fee."
5. (Only if they ask "what's the catch") → "later, if you want, you can
   fund a dare — pay only when verified people show up. but the claim
   is just free."

Rule: never sell the paid product in the first conversation. Claim first,
fund later. The proof trail sells the upgrade by itself.

## What NOT to do
- No paid ads, no follower-buying, no engagement pods — poison for an
  "underground" brand.
- No feature-list posts. One hook, one artifact, per post.
- No posting about features tagged DEMO CAREFULLY / TARGET FLOW in the
  feature guide until their gates clear.
- No "we're live!" launch post. Underground things don't launch; they leak.
