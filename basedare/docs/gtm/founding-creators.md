# BaseDare — Founding Creators Outreach Kit

> Goal: sign up the first **15–30 founding creators in ONE scene (Siargao / General Luna)** against the live funnel: **`basedare.xyz/creators/onboard`**. Concentrate, don't broadcast. A loop that visibly spins in one town beats a thin national list.

## Who to target (and who NOT to)
**Yes — local doers:**
- People already *out* most nights: bartenders, surf instructors, hostel staff, baristas, scooter-tour guides, event/party regulars.
- Micro-creators (500–10k followers) who post real IRL stuff — not polished influencers.
- The "knows everyone in town" connector.

**Map them to mission styles (your Dare DNA):**
| Archetype | Mission styles |
|---|---|
| Surf/beach local | beach-tourism, fitness-stunts |
| Nightlife regular | nightlife, social-proof |
| Foodie / café crawler | food-drink, review-recap |
| Event/party host | event-coverage, group-missions |

**No (for now):** big influencers (expensive, low trust-per-dollar), pure online creators with no IRL presence, anyone who won't physically show up.

## The Founding Creator offer (what they get)
- **Founder badge** — permanent, "I was here first."
- **Signal Points** — reputation that compounds; ranks you up in Creator Radar so venues/brands route *you* first. (Reputation only — no token or cash promise.)
- **First dibs** on paid venue missions (First Spark) as they go live.
- **We subsidize your first 1–2 missions** so you earn real USDC on day one.

## The funnel (where you send them)
**`basedare.xyz/creators/onboard`** → Claim tag → Tune radar (3–5 mission styles + radius) → Set availability → Unlock first missions. ~2 minutes. Tell them to connect a wallet so it saves (Coinbase/Base smart wallet is easiest).

## Objection handling
- *"Is this a scam / what's the catch?"* → No catch. You get paid in USDC for completing real missions; everything's onchain and verifiable. Founding = you're early, so you get the badge + first gigs.
- *"I don't have crypto / a wallet."* → Takes 30s to make a Base smart wallet, no seed phrase. I'll set it up with you. Payouts land instantly.
- *"What do I actually have to do?"* → Show up at a spot, scan a QR / drop proof, done. Real IRL stuff, no cringe content quotas.
- *"How much can I make?"* → Missions vary (think $20–$150+ early). First ones are on us so you see it work.
- *"Points = money?"* → Points are reputation (rank you up for better gigs). No token promise — just real USDC for missions.
