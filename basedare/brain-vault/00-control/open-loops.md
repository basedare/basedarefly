## Risks To Watch

- Paying for disposable posts that do not compound place memory.
- Spreading rewards globally without enough geographic density to make discovery useful.
- Treating paid activity as virality without measuring the organic multiplier.
- Paying creator performance bonuses from browser-local sessions, self-reported referrals, clicks, RSVPs, or proof submissions rather than canonical server-verified completions.
- Losing creator attribution when a participant moves from an Instagram/TikTok webview into Safari, Chrome, a wallet app, or another device.
- Treating creator-mission participation as payout-grade path attribution or causal lift.
- Initiating wallet or social-OAuth execution inside a known social webview instead of handing the participant into an ordinary browser.
- Open bounties attracting farms, collusion, unsafe behavior, or low-quality proof.
- Calling venue-less proof "verified" before submission proximity and fraud economics are proven.
- Selling sponsor reuse rights that the contributor did not explicitly grant.
- Letting bespoke Field Sprints become agency work that does not compound data, templates, reputation, or refresh demand.
- Treating a grant as customer PMF instead of experiment funding.
- Automation spending real funds without explicit caps, pause conditions, and auditability.
- Public pseudonymity drifting into concealed ownership, employment, payment activity, or local commercial control.
- Building events, venue operations, or tourism packages that require founder presence or unlicensed local activity.
