## Quality Bar

A proof counts as useful only if it has:

- verified or explicitly review-routed proximity and freshness
- unique evidence tied to one structured question
- sufficient rights for the promised deliverable
- an `as of` date and refresh expectation
- a durable place-memory record and receipt path

## Weekly Review

Questions to answer at each review:

- Can a cold contributor claim, prove, and get paid without a founder or pre-built identity?
- What percentage of proof cleared automatically versus Sentinel review, and at what cost?
- Which fraud attempts or disputes changed the next template?
- Did the Sprint create reusable records and refresh demand, or only a bespoke report?
- Did ecosystem funding advance without being confused with customer PMF?
- Did the design partner accept the receipt, and did the next buyer pay?
