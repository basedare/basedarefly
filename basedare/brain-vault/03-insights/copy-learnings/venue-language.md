# Venue Language

## Working Hypothesis

Venue outreach should lead with "verified local moments" and "creator proof at your place" before using heavier platform language.

## Prefer

- "verified proof at your venue"
- "local route"
- "creator check-in"
- "first venue mission"
- "make the place feel alive on the map"
- "Spark Audit"

## Avoid Early

- "Web3 activation"
- "AI autonomous growth system"
- "guaranteed traffic"
- "viral campaign"
- "fully automated venue funnel"
