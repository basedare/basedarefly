# Creator recruitment copy — Siargao (founding cohort)

Approved, founder-voice copy for recruiting the **first creators in Siargao**. This is the single source for the creator-recruitment voice — build messaging *from* this, don't reinvent it. (Captured 2026-06-29.)

## Positioning principle (read first)
**Market this like an invite, not a startup launch.** "First 10 creators in Siargao" should feel **selective, local, and real**.
- Lead with the offer, not the platform: *show up → post the night → bring people → get paid for verified arrivals.*
- **"No wallet needed to apply."** Wallet/passport comes second.
- **No fake urgency, no fake counters** ("3/10 spots left" is banned — matches the no-fake-numbers rule on `/markets`).
- It's "paid local missions," explicitly **not** "an influencer ad thing."

**Funnel target:** all links point at the live intake form → `https://basedare.xyz/creators/signup?city=siargao`
(That page is city-aware: shows the "Founding cohort: Siargao / General Luna" line, and applying needs no wallet.)
