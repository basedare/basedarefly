# Siargao venue-night map (founder field intel, updated 2026-07-14)

Source: founder (mrrobot13) — direct conversations with bar owners + island presence.

## The weekly rotation (General Luna)

Each venue "owns" a night. This is the island's existing social operating system —
BaseDare doesn't have to create a calendar, it has to plug into one.

**Nightly late option:** Siargao Beach Club (SBC) runs every night until about
3am. This is a late fallback, not a replacement for the main rotating venue.

Occasional raves and special events happen monthly. They are date-specific and
must come from the live Tonight/event layer when confirmed; they must not be
invented from this weekly convention.

Some nights are softer/less defined than others — the rotation is convention, not
contract, and it CAN be disrupted (a new "owned night" is winnable). Product copy
must label this as the **usual rhythm**, never as a guarantee that a venue is open,
busy, or hosting a specific event tonight.

## Demand signal

Bar owners report **overall low foot traffic recently** (founder conversations,
June 2026). This is the venue-side pain that makes "fund a Mission Night /
buy verified visits" land NOW rather than as a nice-to-have.

## Founder leverage

Strong island network including police and politicians — permits/safety/officialdom
de-risked for IRL events.

## Implications for the machine

1. **Mission Night scheduling = counter-programming or co-programming.** Either
   pitch the venue that owns the night ("make your night provable") or pitch an
   emerging venue the EMPTY night slot (e.g., a quiet Monday/Thursday alternative).
2. **The weekly rotation is the product calendar after intent.** Once a visitor
   explicitly asks what is on tonight, map states can follow this table before
   any venue signs. Do not surface a named nightly venue before the visitor
   answers the next-two-hours question; live counts may remain neutral.
3. **Emerging venues (Gaya, Lost) are the First Spark beachhead** — low foot
   traffic + no owned night + ambition = exact ICP for "Fill a slow hour. Get proof."
4. Hostel Sunday (Happiness) confirms the hostel-as-wedge thesis with a concrete
   anchor night.
