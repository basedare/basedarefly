# Siargao / General Luna Signal

## Statement

General Luna is a strong first map-memory market because the same visitor can move through surf spots, cafes, beach clubs, hostels, island-hopping docks, and nightlife in one dense local loop.

## Evidence

- Current BaseDare map work already includes multiple venue categories in a compact area.
- Pin accuracy has visibly affected trust in the product experience.
- The venue layer can support both creator proof and tourist route discovery.

## Applies To

- Venue scouting
- Creator route design
- Spark Audit drafts
- Push notification relevance
- Map density planning

## Behavioral Rule

Prioritize clusters of venues that can form a route. A single isolated pin is less powerful than a loop that gets people moving.
