## Pre-loaded answer — incrementality ("would they have come anyway?")

> **"How do you know they wouldn't have come anyway?"** (venue / skeptic)

We do **not** claim perfect incrementality on Night 1 — and we say so. We prove **attribution, not incrementality**: this person came through the creator/source path, **checked in during the window** (QR/GPS), and was **co-confirmed by the venue** at reconciliation. Attribution is provable; incrementality is not — one night can't isolate counterfactual demand. The honest line is *"here's who we can prove the creator sent,"* never *"here's the lift."* (Lock; see `docs/PHILOSOPHY.md` → Night-1 measurement rule.)
