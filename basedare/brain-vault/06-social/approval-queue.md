# Social Approval Queue

> The boundary: **Fable prepares, a human publishes.** No AI ever posts, replies, DMs,
> or makes a promise publicly. This is not a temporary training wheel — it is the design.

## Pipeline

```
ARTIFACT → DRAFT → FACT-CHECK → APPROVED → POSTED → MEASURED
```

| Stage | Who | What happens |
|---|---|---|
| **ARTIFACT** | founder (or a player/buyer, with consent) | A real thing exists: a receipt, a completed mission, a shipped feature, a new map spot, a buyer answer. No artifact → no post. |
| **DRAFT** | Fable (content-operator skill) | Produces the weekly package (max 3 posts) from the canon + artifact only. Every factual sentence carries a source. |
| **FACT-CHECK** | Fable then founder | Each claim checked against `content-canon.md` allowed/prohibited lists; numbers re-read from the live product and dated. Anything unsourced gets cut, not softened. |
| **APPROVED** | founder only | Founder edits/approves in this file. Approval is per-post, not per-week. |
| **POSTED** | founder, manually | Posted by hand on the platform. Paste the final URL below. |
| **MEASURED** | founder + Fable | Metrics logged in `performance-ledger.md` at ~48h and ~7d; verdict recorded. |

## Hard rules

1. **No autonomous publishing.** Drafting is automatic; posting/replying/DMing is human.
2. **Max 3 artifact-led posts per week.** Scarcity forces quality; no filler content.
3. **Every factual claim has a source** from the canon's allowed list. New claim → add it
   to the canon (with source) first, or cut it.
4. **Rights gate:** the artifact's asset must be in the canon's asset register with
   rights = owned/consented and a fresh `as of` date.
5. **Public promises are review-required, always** — pricing, timelines, guarantees,
   partnerships, anything a stranger could hold us to.
6. **Kill fast:** if fact-check flags a post, it dies this week. It can come back next
   week fixed. Never "post now, correct later."
