# Siargao Venue Pilot Loop

## Objective

Turn the improved Siargao map into real venue conversations and one lightweight pilot.

## Hypothesis

If BaseDare offers venue-specific Spark Audits to the best Siargao venue leads, then at least one operator will engage because the audit is concrete, local, and low-commitment.

## Segment

General Luna and Catangnan venues with visible tourist, surf, food, or nightlife flow.

## Variant A

Lead with creator proof and venue memory.

## Variant B

Lead with tourist route discovery and local movement.

## Metric

Primary: warm replies from qualified venues.

Secondary:

- audits requested
- calls booked
- pilot interest
- objections captured

## Success Threshold

One warm reply or one call booked from the first five qualified venues.

## Next Action

Review the first Spark Audit invite draft and choose the first three venue targets.
