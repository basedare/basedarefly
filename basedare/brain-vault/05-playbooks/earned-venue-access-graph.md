# Earned Venue Access Graph

## Thesis

BaseDare can become a trusted map of **who can open which real-world doors, under what authority, and why that access was earned**.

The loop is:

`contribute useful proof → build trust → unlock a request or perk → help a venue/player → create an attributable arrival or receipt → earn deeper local access`

This relationship graph is a possible long-term moat because it joins verified contribution, venue authorization, intent, and measurable real-world outcomes. It is not a generic directory, influencer marketplace, coupon club, or purchased VIP scheme.

## Product rules

### Official venue contact routes

Claimed venues may publish preferred contact routes for distinct purposes:

- WhatsApp
- Instagram
- Messenger
- phone
- email
- reservation or booking link
- partnership or event path

Each route records purpose, response hours, source, consent basis, verification status, last confirmation, and whether it is public. Public business routes or details published by the official venue are acceptable. Personal details require explicit consent. Stale, revoked, private, or unverified routes do not appear publicly.

The public surface is **Connect with venue** inside the selected venue profile. It is not another floating map control. Showing an official contact never implies that the venue is a BaseDare partner.

### Separate identity from authority

| Identity | How it is gained | What it permits | What it never permits |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mayor | Earned verified contribution at a place | Public contribution/reputation signal | Speaking for the venue, ownership, settlement, self-approval |
| Ambassador | Explicit venue authorization | Scoped welcoming, questions, approved introductions or perks | Venue ownership, settlement, approving own paid proof |
| Host | Explicit authorization for one activity | Run the named gathering, mission, route, or Spark Window | Permanent representation or unrelated venue operations |

Ambassador and Host authorizations must be scoped, timestamped, revocable, and attributable to an authorized venue actor. A strong Mayor may be invited into an Ambassador circle, but the promotion is never automatic.

### Earned access is two-dimensional

The working ladder is:

1. **Explorer** — official contacts and public place information.
2. **Proven Player** — basic perks and structured venue questions.
3. **Local Key** — request trusted introductions or selected experiences.
4. **Venue Circle** — venue-approved ambassador, host, or collaborator.

An unlock considers both:

- global reliability, contribution quality, conduct, no-shows, and disputes; and
- venue-specific history, verified presence, useful contribution, and explicit venue approval.

A level unlocks the ability to request access. It never guarantees a response, booking, collaboration, creator attention, or admission.

### Concierge follows declared intent

Before routing a contact or person, ask what the player needs:

- somewhere social tonight
- a reservation
- local advice
- a collaboration
- an event contact
- route help

Only then suggest the relevant official route, authorized Ambassador, Host, or opted-in creator. No unsolicited introductions. No open city chat. No cold-DM marketplace.

### Perks must create measurable venue value

Early candidates:

- welcome drink or small upgrade
- off-peak discount
- priority RSVP
- secret-menu item
- reserved community table
- approved gathering access
- collaboration request path
- higher-value venue mission

Stronger unlocks require a rotating QR or BaseDare Secure Handshake. Track verified arrival, redemption, repeat visit, and receipt. Reputation is never sold; the venue or sponsor funds the benefit.

## Authorization and conflict rules

- Claimed venue wallet or BaseDare moderator may publish official contact routes.
- Personal contact routes require explicit consent at publication.
- Mayor cannot grant venue authorization.
- Ambassador/Host cannot approve their own paid proof.
- No relationship can change venue ownership or a settlement wallet.
- Revocation takes effect immediately for future access.
- Public routes fail closed on missing, stale, revoked, or uncertain verification.
- Do not claim a partnership without a separate explicit agreement.

## Pilot: three venues, not a platform-wide hierarchy

Recruit three claimed venues. For each:

1. verify one or more official contact routes;
2. appoint one venue-approved Ambassador;
3. configure one modest earned perk;
4. allow Proven Players to make one structured introduction request;
5. require handshake/redemption evidence for stronger benefits;
6. collect the venue/player outcome and complaint signal.

### Pilot scorecard

- contact opens
- contact-to-response rate
- median response time
- structured introduction requests
- accepted introductions
- verified arrivals
- perk redemptions
- repeat visits within 30 days
- venue repeat intent
- player complaints or privacy incidents
- ambassador/host revocations

Do not generalize the access ladder until all three venues can operate it without founder presence and without confusing Mayor reputation with venue authority.

## Monetization boundary

Official public contact routes remain free because they improve map utility and discovery.

A later venue partner product may test `$149–$299/month` for:

- verified profile and preferred routing
- Ambassador seats and permissions
- gated perk configuration
- concierge-request inbox
- tracked handshake redemptions
- attributed venue visits
- recurring missions
- monthly proof and conversion receipts

This range is exploratory, not financial canon. It does not replace the first commercial SKU: the `$2,500 Verified Field Sprint`. Managed missions remain separately priced; the 4% self-serve settlement fee is not a managed-service fee.

## Build order

### Now

- provenance-backed official contact records
- owner/admin write boundary
- verified-only public **Connect with venue** surface
- one public-business-contact seed for UI and policy validation

### After three venue commitments

- scoped Ambassador/Host authorization ledger
- structured intent request object
- one handshake-backed perk per venue
- operator scorecard and complaint path

### Only after pilot evidence

- Explorer / Proven Player / Local Key / Venue Circle productization
- venue subscription experiment
- opt-in creator introduction routing
- broader concierge automation

## Explicitly not building now

- purchasable reputation
- guaranteed introductions
- an unmoderated people directory
- open city chat
- automatic Mayor-to-Ambassador promotion
- Ambassador settlement or proof-review authority
- generic coupons detached from verified presence
