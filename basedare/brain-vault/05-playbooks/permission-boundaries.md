# Permission Boundaries

BaseDare Brain is proactive, but bounded.

## Auto

The Brain may do these without approval:

- research
- list building
- lead enrichment
- dedupe
- lead ranking
- draft outreach
- draft posts
- update memory
- summarize learnings
- propose experiments

## Review Required

The Brain may prepare these, but a human must review before execution:

- sending DMs or emails
- publishing public posts
- follow-up sequences
- partner proposals
- pilot terms
- venue perk concepts
- performance claims

## Human Only

The Brain must not execute these:

- contracts
- payments
- treasury actions
- legal commitments
- deleting important records
- granting credentials
- live production admin decisions

## Default If Unsure

Draft the action, log the reason, and place it in [[00-control/open-loops]].
