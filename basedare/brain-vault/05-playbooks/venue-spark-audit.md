# Venue Spark Audit

The Spark Audit is the preferred first lead magnet for venue acquisition.

## Purpose

Give a venue a short, concrete plan for how BaseDare could make their place more alive through verified creator proof, local missions, and check-ins.

## Required Sections

1. Venue
2. City
3. Buyer goal
4. Local signal
5. Five mission ideas
6. Creator archetypes
7. QR or check-in mechanic
8. Proof metrics
9. Recommended pilot offer
10. One next action

## Rules

- Generate audits automatically as drafts.
- Do not promise traffic, revenue, payouts, or implementation timelines.
- Attach the audit summary to the venue lead before outreach.
- Use objections and replies to improve future audits.
