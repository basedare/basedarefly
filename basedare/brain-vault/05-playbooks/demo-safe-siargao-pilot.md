# Demo-Safe Siargao Pilot

This playbook turns the current map polish into a safe growth loop.

## Demo Surface

Use `/map` with the Siargao venue cluster visible.

Before showing it:

- confirm the top nav does not overlap quick picks
- confirm Take Proof, Fund Dare, and Open Venue buttons fit
- confirm Cloud 9, Tuason Point, and Malinao are not visibly misplaced
- open one venue panel and show the proof, check-in, and local chat surfaces

## Target Order

1. [[01-leads/venues/siargao-beach-club]]
2. [[01-leads/venues/hideaway]]
3. [[01-leads/venues/cloud-9-boardwalk]]

## What To Say

BaseDare helps places become visible through verified local moments: check-ins, creator proof, small missions, and map memory.

## What Not To Say

- Do not promise guaranteed traffic.
- Do not promise revenue.
- Do not describe it as a complex Web3 campaign first.
- Do not imply QR is required for every action.

## Review Package

Before outreach, prepare:

- the venue lead note
- one Spark Audit
- one short invite message
- one expected objection
- one next action if they reply

## Success Signal

The first win is not a contract. The first win is a real operator saying, "Send the idea" or "Show me how this would work here."
