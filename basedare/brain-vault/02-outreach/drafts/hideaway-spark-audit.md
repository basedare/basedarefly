# Hideaway Spark Audit

## Vibe Signal

Hideaway can work as a boardwalk and island-hopping afterflow venue. The best angle is not "download an app"; it is "turn tonight's movement around the dock into proof people can follow."

## Mission Ideas

1. First group proof after an island-hopping return.
2. Best sunset drink proof.
3. Bring a new visitor to Hideaway.
4. Local guide check-in mission.
5. Boardwalk route completion: dock, Hideaway, next stop.

## Creator Archetypes

1. Tour guide or boat crew with recurring tourist contact.
2. Travel creator documenting a full Siargao day.
3. Nightlife local who can make the route feel real.

## QR Or Check-In Mechanic

Use a QR at the counter for a time-window mission tied to island-hopping return hours or evening boardwalk traffic.

## Proof Metrics

- group proofs
- check-ins during return window
- route completions
- creator mentions
- first-time visitor count if tracked manually

## Recommended Offer

Start with one "after island hopping" proof mission and a simple recap screenshot. Keep staff work close to zero.

## Audit Summary

Hideaway's best pilot is a route-based activation. BaseDare should sell the feeling of local movement, not a complicated loyalty system.

## Buyer Next Action

Confirm the best contact path and ask if they want a one-page boardwalk activation idea.
