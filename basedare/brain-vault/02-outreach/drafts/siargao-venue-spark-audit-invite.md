# Siargao Venue Spark Audit Invite

## Objective

Open a real venue conversation without promising traffic, money, or technical setup.

## Personalization Hook

BaseDare is building a Siargao venue-memory map where visitors and creators leave verified proof at local places. The first useful offer is a small Spark Audit: a concrete activation plan for one venue.

## Draft

Hey [Name] - I am building BaseDare around Siargao venues and creator proof.

The simple idea: people can discover local spots, check in, complete small venue missions, and leave proof that makes the place feel alive on the map.

I wanted to make you a quick Spark Audit for [Venue]: 5 lightweight activation ideas, the best creator types to route there, and what a first QR/check-in pilot could look like.

No pitch deck, no big integration. Just a useful first plan you can approve, ignore, or reshape.

Would it be okay if I send the short audit over?

## Why This Should Work

- It asks permission before sending a bigger plan.
- It offers venue-specific value instead of a generic app pitch.
- It avoids promises about guaranteed traffic or revenue.
- It fits the current Siargao map work.
