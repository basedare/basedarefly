# Siargao Beach Club Spark Audit

## Vibe Signal

Beach-club and nightlife energy is a strong first BaseDare venue loop because people already want to prove they were there, bring friends, and post the moment.

## Mission Ideas

1. First verified night check-in after 9pm.
2. Best table-energy proof clip of the night.
3. Bring three friends and leave one group proof.
4. DJ drop reaction shot.
5. Golden-hour-to-night transition proof.

## Creator Archetypes

1. Nightlife micro-creator who posts stories in General Luna.
2. Travel creator looking for the best night-out stop.
3. Local host or guide who brings groups through town.

## QR Or Check-In Mechanic

Run a simple venue-console QR at the bar or entrance. Visitors scan to unlock a venue-specific proof mission. GPS remains broad proximity; QR confirms presence.

## Proof Metrics

- verified check-ins
- proofs submitted
- unique creators
- repeat visits
- best proof clip selected by staff or BaseDare operator

## Recommended Offer

Start with a one-night pilot: one QR, one mission, one recap. Do not promise traffic; measure whether people understand and use the mechanic.

## Audit Summary

Siargao Beach Club is the strongest first nightlife pilot because the venue already has social energy. BaseDare should position this as "make the night visible on the map," not as a generic Web3 campaign.

## Buyer Next Action

Ask permission to send this short audit, then offer a 15-minute walkthrough if they want to try one night.
